NovaStack Digital

We build clarity, craft, and conversion for product-first teams.

Founded in 2014, NovaStack is a distributed studio of strategists, engineers, and design leaders who turn complex problems into lovable products. We exist to help ambitious teams scale responsibly while staying wildly creative.

Origin: Started in a Brooklyn loft with three clients and a bet that product strategy should feel as premium as the experiences we ship.

Today: Operating across six studios with partners in fintech, health, climate, and deep tech.

Our Story

From pop-up studio to global product partner.

We began as a small research pod helping founders translate insight into experiments. A decade later, we embed with scale-ups and enterprises to modernize their entire product chains.

We believe the best products come from teams who are grounded in reality, obsessed with craft, and optimistic about what technology can unlock.

2014

Launch

NovaStack opens as a two-week experiment blending venture strategy and product design.

2016

First international studio

We expand to Lisbon to tap into nearshore engineering talent.

2019

Platform practice

Launched our systems team to help clients move from monoliths to composable stacks.

2022

Impact lab

Introduced climate and health venture labs to scale meaningful innovation.

2024

Global collective

Now 6 studios, 14 partners, and clients on 4 continents.
